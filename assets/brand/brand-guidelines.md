# Eastokyo Magazine brand assets

## Brand files
- `logo.svg` — primary dark logo for light backgrounds.
- `logo-dark.svg` — dark logo for light backgrounds.
- `logo-light.svg` — white logo for dark backgrounds.
- `symbol.svg` — crow and ink-circle symbol without words.
- `wordmark.svg` — Eastokyo Magazine wordmark without the crow.

## Icon files
- `../icons/favicon.svg`
- `../icons/favicon.ico` — contains 16 × 16, 32 × 32 and 48 × 48 icons.
- `../icons/favicon-16x16.png`
- `../icons/favicon-32x32.png`
- `../icons/favicon-48x48.png`
- `../icons/apple-touch-icon.png` — 180 × 180.
- `../icons/android-chrome-192.png` — 192 × 192.
- `../icons/android-chrome-512.png` — 512 × 512.
- `../icons/mstile-150.png` — 150 × 150.
- `../icons/site.webmanifest`

## Usage
Do not stretch, rotate, recolour, rearrange or add effects.
Use `logo-light.svg` only on dark backgrounds.
The favicon must remain free of letters and words.

## Artwork handling
The approved ink artwork is preserved inside the SVG files rather than automatically traced.
This prevents the crow, brush texture and ink circle from becoming blocky or distorted.
