About the magazine

Designed in Tokyo, not designed about Japan.

Eastokyo is an independent, photography-led magazine devoted exclusively to East Tokyo.

Its visual language is bold, warm, and expressive. Its subject is local, specific, and real—without tourist shorthand or borrowed clichés.
